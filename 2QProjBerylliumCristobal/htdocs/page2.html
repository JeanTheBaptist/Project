Organization

This page talks about the organization and different branches of Tempest, and also shows the hierarchy of power Tempest has.

The Cabinet

Aside from King Rimuru, The Cabinet has the highest authority of all affairs aside from war. It has multiple ministries and the Prime Minister is Rigurd.

The Ministry of Defense

Minister: Benimaru

Encompasses the elite military branches of Tempest. Currently in the anime, it includes the following squads; Green Numbers, the Yellow Numbers, Hiryuu, the Goblin Riders, the Shadow Squad, Kurenai and Yomigaeri.

The Ministry of Intelligence

Minister: Souei

Is the group which does espionage and information gathering. They are partly in the military, as the Shadow Squad are the main members of this ministry.

The Ministry of Infrastructure

Minister: Geld

Is responsible for creating the buildings of Tempest and also for expanding it. It is mostly made up of the orcs and labourers who joined after the Orc War.

The Ministry of Science

Minister: Benimaru

Is responsible for advancing the technology, potions and weapons of Tempest. It includes both the ingredient gatherers, and researchers like Vestia.

The Ministry of Treasury and Finances

Minister: Gard Mjollmill

Is responsible for recording the finances of Tempest. It is also assumed that this ministry is responsible for the trades and merchandising Tempest does.

The Military

The Military Branches of Tempest are responsible for fending off the nation's foes. It is headed by Benimaru, and is also called the Ministry of Defense.

Kurenai

Leader: Benimaru

This unit consists of the 400 Goblins from the original Goblin Village at the start of Tempest's growth. These Goblins had evolved into Ogres and are the private bodyguards of General Benimaru and are currently the strongest unit.

Hiryuu

Leader: Sir Gabiru

This unit consists of 100 elite Dragonewts evolved from the Lizardfolk village. These soldiers are masters of aerial combat.

Yomigaeri

Leader: Shion

This unit consists of the 100 Hobgoblins revived by Rimuru, along with Shion. These soldiers are elites, who constantly regenerate.

Goblin Riders

Leader: Gobta

This unit consists of 100 Hobgoblins who each mount a Star Wolf, and is the first unit created in Tempest. In battle, they act as cavalry.

The Green and Yellow Numbers

Leader: Geld and Gobta

The Green Numbers, led by Gobta and Yellow Numbers, led by Geld, are the basic units of Tempest. They each have 12,000 and 2,000 units respectively.
