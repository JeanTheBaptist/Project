Website and TenSura

The Jura Hub is a website where you can learn more about the Jura Tempest Federation, a fictional nation in TenSura. Tensei Shitara Slime Datta Ken (Tensura), is a light novel written by Fuse and drawn by Mitz Vah. It's about a human who reincarnates into another world as a slime.

What is Jura Tempest?

The Jura Tempest Federation, also known as Tempest, is the nation owned and founded by Rimuru Tempest, the protagonist slime of TenSura. Throughout the series, we see it grow and prosper along with Rimuru as the story goes on.
